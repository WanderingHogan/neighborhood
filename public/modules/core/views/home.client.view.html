BeLocate helps you objectively compare different places, be they homes to rent, homes to buy, locations for a business, or detailed neighborhood information before booking a hotel. We provide up-to-date, highly detailed reports, maps, and data about locations you are about to invest your time and money in.

We help you quickly do your homework before investing in a new place.

People

Find out detailed, local information about the people near the locations you are interested in, including census and other local population datasets.

Emergency

See where each location is, relative to crime and emergency services information, sex offendors, distance to hospitals and emergency medical facilities, and more.

Environment

Information about positives like nearby parks, greenspace, urban farms. Also, information about brownfields, superfund sites, and even local environmental hazards like radon likelihood.

Transportation

Information about local public transportation, walkability, traffic, estimated rush hour commutes from each of the locations you compare with several points of interest

Community & Education

Schools and Ratings, nearby parks and ammenities, neighborhood and civic associations and contact, among other attributes.

Entertainment

Nearby restaurants, bars, night-life, cultural-events, and more fun things.
